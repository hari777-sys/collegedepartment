about department

The department was established in 1987 with UG Computer Science. It started M.Sc. Computer Science in 1999 and PGDCA in 2000. The syllabus is a mixture of both theory and practical. The department has well equipped laboratories with the state of art technology and other facilities. It has produced quality professionals holding key positions in IT companies around the world.
