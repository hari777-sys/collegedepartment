about department

The department's journey began in 1987 with the introduction of Undergraduate (UG) Computer Science courses. Over the years, it expanded its offerings, launching M.Sc. Computer Science in 1999 and PGDCA (Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications) in 2000. The department's curriculum strikes a balance between theoretical knowledge and practical application, empowering students with comprehensive skills.

To facilitate effective learning, the department boasts well-equipped laboratories with cutting-edge technology and modern facilities. This ensures that students have hands-on experience and exposure to the latest advancements in the field.

The department takes pride in its alumni, who have excelled in their careers and assumed significant roles in various Information Technology (IT) companies worldwide. The quality professionals produced by the department continue to make a positive impact on the IT industry, holding key positions and driving innovation.

With a rich history of growth and excellence, the department remains committed to nurturing the next generation of computer science professionals and empowering them to thrive in the dynamic and evolving IT landscape.
